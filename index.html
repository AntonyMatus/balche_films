01.

Development

In the initial project stage, spanning months to years, we meticulously handle idea development, drafting, production logistics, and funding. The screenwriter leads this creative phase, which we thoroughly enjoy.

02.

Pre-Production

After securing funding and a greenlight, the producer and director prepare for filming by finalizing the budget, script, and logistics. They are now fully ready for a smooth shoot.

03.

Production

The film production phase: we trust the director's creative control, prioritize collaboration, and aim to connect with global audiences, irrespective of budget or crew size.

04.

Post-Production

In the final stage, we edit footage, refine sound, and add effects. After approval, the film is locked for distribution. Careful post-production is crucial for a lasting impact.
